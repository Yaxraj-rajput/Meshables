# Meshables - 3D Assets Marketplace for AR/VR & Game Developers 

Welcome to **Meshables**, the one-stop-shop for all your **Aassets Needs**, whether you're building the next big thing in **game development**, **AR/VR experiences**, or just need that one perfect model to make your digital garden look *extra fancy* 🌸. We’ve got you covered.

### Why Meshables?
Because who needs just *another* platform with a few overused assets? Here, it's **all community-driven**, so you'll never feel like you're caught in a digital monopoly. Oh, and did we mention you can trade assets like it’s a stock exchange for 3D models? More on that below! 

## Features That Make Us Cool (and Useful)

- **3D Asset Marketplace**: Models for **AR**, **VR**, and **game development**, from vases to full-blown city landscapes. If it exists in 3D, it's probably here.
- **Blender Plugin**: Export and import directly between **Blender** and Meshables, because who has time for extra steps? 
- **Trade Feature**: Tired of just buying and downloading assets? **Trade them** with other creators and developers like it's the stock market! Put those negotiation skills to use and score some killer deals.
- **Group Buy Feature**: Teams can now **purchase assets together**, so everyone gets access. Sharing is caring, right?
- **Group Asset Access**: No more buying the same asset 5 times! If your team’s got it, you’ve got it.
- **Individual Previewers**: You get a **personalized previewer** for each type of asset: 
  - **3D models**: Spin, zoom, and marvel at every polygon. 
  - **HDRIs**: Check out the lighting setup before you commit to downloading.
  - **Textures**: View those sweet, high-res patterns in all their glory. 
  - **Printable Files**: Make sure it fits your printer setup. No surprises, just clean prints. 
  - **Code Files**: Review and run snippets right in the browser! No mystery code here. 
- **AR/VR Ready**: Yup, all the assets here are primed for your futuristic projects. 
- **Cross-Platform Support**: We don’t pick favorites. Whether you’re on Windows, macOS, or just trying to run things on Linux, it’ll work.
- **Discord Bot Integration**: Need assets on the go? Just ask our **Discord bot** to fetch them for you. It’s like DoorDash, but for 3D models.

## How It Works?

1. **Sign up** and explore our vast library of 3D assets. You might even stumble upon something that you didn’t know you needed. 
2. **Search, Preview, and Download** - Each asset type has its own **previewer**, so you’ll know exactly what you’re getting before you hit download.
3. **Trade Assets** - Have a 3D model you no longer need but someone else is craving? **Trade it** for something cool or useful. Or just because you like making deals. 
4. **Group Purchases** - Why buy individually when you can split the bill with your team? Work smarter, not harder!
5. **Use Our Discord Bot** - Type a few commands in Discord, get 3D assets delivered to your project. No need to leave your comfy chat window.
